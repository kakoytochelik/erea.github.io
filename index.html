About me
Welcome! My name is Alexey Eremeev, thank you for your interest in my portfolio! This site provides quick links to my projects. This site is also part of my portfolio and was created with HTML (Bootstrap Grid), CSS (Sass), JavaScript (jQuery). All cards are tapable: first tap - information, second tap - go to the link, scroll or swipe - hide the information. In 2020, I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Mechatronics and Robotics from the Russian Technical University (RTU MIREA). On the basis of my thesis about applying Neural Networks with image processing, I created a Telegram-bot for processing (deblurring using Neural network) and creating panoramic images. Unfortunately, this bot is currently offline. From 2019 to 2020 I worked as manual QA engineer in VK holding company's service testing program called "/testpool". Since 2021 I have been working as a QA and Automatization engineer and since 2023 as a QA TeamLead at 1C International. We use an internal framework based on Cucumber as well as SikuliX, Appium and Cypress to test desktop, mobile, and web version of an ERP product called 1C:Drive. I have knowledge in HTML, CSS, preprocessors (Sass, Scss, Less), JavaScript, Bootstrap library, can apply Bootstrap-Grid to create adaptive websites. Have knowledge of Swift and Kotlin basics. I can create Python scripts and GUI software (using PyQT, for example) in addition to Robot Operating System and CoppeliaSim. Also have some experience in Unity and Unreal Engine. But I believe that the most important skill is to be able to search for information and apply it to the task at hand. Why reinvent the wheel?
